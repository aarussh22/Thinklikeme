About Me

Hello! I’m a passionate chess enthusiast who believes in the power of strategic thinking to transform lives. I created Think Like Me as a space to combine creativity, logic, and fun. With every move on the board, I aim to teach how critical decision-making, foresight, and resilience apply not just to chess — but to life itself.

Whether you're just getting started or looking to sharpen your tactical edge, I’m here to share insights, tools, and inspiration to help you level up your mind.
